The Azari Family Legacy

Founded in 1974 by Michael Azari, an Israeli immigrant with Panamanian heritage, Coral has grown from a small Miami warehouse to America's 3rd largest shrimp importer.

Today, brothers Jeff, Dan, and David Azari continue their father's vision, bringing millions of pounds of premium seafood from the world's finest fishing fleets and farmers to American tables.

Our commitment to excellence spans 50 years, built on relationships with suppliers from the Gulf of Mexico to the Pacific Ocean, ensuring every product meets our family's uncompromising standards.
