Discover Our Story

The Azari Family Legacy

From a small Miami warehouse to America's 3rd largest shrimp importer

1974

The Beginning

Founded by Michael Azari, an Israeli immigrant with Panamanian heritage, who started with a singular vision: to bring the world's finest seafood to American tables. From a modest Miami warehouse, an empire was born.

1980s

Expansion Era

Growth across Florida with the opening of our Tampa location, establishing a strong presence in the Southeast seafood market. The force of expansion was unstoppable.

2000s

New Generation

Brothers Jeff, Dan, and David Azari take leadership, bringing innovation and expanding our global sourcing network. A new era of leadership begins.

2024

Industry Leader

Today we stand as America's 3rd largest shrimp importer, with $600M+ in annual import value and 11,037+ global shipments. The vision realized.
